Junteng Liu is a first-year Ph.D. candidate at the HKUST NLP Group, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Junxian He. His research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning, with specific interests in LLM reasoning and reinforcement learning, hallucination in vision-language models, and LLM truthfulness and interpretability. He graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University with a B.Eng. in 2024. He has interned at MINIMAX, Tencent WXG, and Shanghai AI Lab. His publications include first-author papers at EMNLP, ICML, and NeurIPS.
